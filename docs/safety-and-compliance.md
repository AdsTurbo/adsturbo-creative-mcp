# Safety and Compliance

[简体中文](safety-and-compliance.zh-CN.md)

AdsTurbo Creative MCP creates planning artifacts. It does not decide whether an ad is legal, compliant, or platform-approved.

## Creative references

Use references for:

- Structure
- Pacing
- Shot order
- Caption density
- Narrative pattern
- Inspiration

Do not use references to copy:

- Protected creative assets
- Finished ads
- Brand identities
- Creator likenesses
- Trademarks
- Claims
- Voice or persona

## Claims

Generated scripts should stay close to product facts supplied by the user.

Review extra carefully when a script includes:

- Guaranteed results
- Medical or health claims
- Financial claims
- Superiority claims such as "best" or "#1"
- Before/after claims
- Scarcity claims
- Testimonials or review claims

Use `forbiddenClaims` in the input when the team knows certain claims must not appear.

## Rights and likeness

Do not use this tool to impersonate people, creators, competitors, public figures, or brands without permission.

If a plan references an actor, creator, reviewer, or customer, confirm that rights are cleared before production.

## Platform policy

Each ad platform has its own rules. Review the final script, storyboard, captions, claims, and landing page before publishing.

This tool is not legal advice, medical advice, financial advice, or platform-policy advice.
